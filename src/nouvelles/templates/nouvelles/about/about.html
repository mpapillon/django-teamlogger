About TeamLogger

What is TeamLogger

TeamLogger enhance the spread of valuable work or infos about a projet. It focus on teamspirit and stress the importance of sharing between team members in order to keep a high level of transparency.

How can I help?

You can share your ideas or encountered issues on the GitHub.
If you are a developer your help is welcome !
